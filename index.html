Privacy Policy – CuberUp

This privacy policy explains how the mobile game CuberUp handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

CuberUp does not collect personal information directly. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may automatically collect limited data, including:

  • Device type and OS version
  • Advertising ID (Ad ID)
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • App usage statistics (crash logs, session time, etc.)

2. How We Use the Information

The collected information may be used to:

  • Improve game performance
  • Display personalized or non-personalized ads
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Third-Party Services

CuberUp uses the following services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

Please refer to their privacy policies for more details: Google Privacy Policy

4. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.
